<p><p>The laser-beam welding consumables market includes various types: stick electrodes, solid wires, flux-cored wires, and submerged arc welding (SAW) wires and fluxes. Stick electrodes are used for manual welding, providing a portable option. Solid wires are often employed in automatic processes for consistent feed and ease of use. Flux-cored wires combine solid wire with a flux material for improved penetration and weld quality. SAW wires and fluxes facilitate deep welds in thicker materials, enhancing productivity and efficiency in industrial applications.</p></p>
