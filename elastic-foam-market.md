<p><ul><li>Obturator</li><li>Opening</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elastic foam market can be categorized into two main types: obturator and opening. </p><p>Obturator foam is designed to provide a seal, primarily used in applications that require sound dampening or thermal insulation. It effectively blocks air, moisture, or contaminants, enhancing comfort and energy efficiency.</p><p>Opening foam, conversely, is engineered for applications where airflow or flexibility is necessary. This type allows for movement and expansion, making it ideal for cushioning in furniture, vehicles, and packaging, while still maintaining a degree of support and resilience.</p></p>
