<p><strong>The Softgel Tumble Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Tumble Drying</li><li>Continuous Tumble Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The softgel tumble dryer market features two primary types: standard tumble drying and continuous tumble drying. Standard tumble drying involves batch processing, where softgel capsules are dried in separate cycles, allowing for thorough moisture removal and control over drying conditions. In contrast, continuous tumble drying streamlines the process, enabling a constant flow of softgel capsules through a drying chamber, enhancing efficiency and reducing drying time. Both methods aim to achieve optimal moisture content for softgel stability and quality.</p></p>
