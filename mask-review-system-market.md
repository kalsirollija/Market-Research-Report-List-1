<p><strong>The Mask Review System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Review System market encompasses two primary types: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB) methods. The DD method involves direct comparisons between two specific masks, focusing on identifying defects and ensuring quality in semiconductor manufacturing. Conversely, the DB method leverages a database of previously reviewed masks to conduct assessments, streamlining defect detection by referencing historical data. Both methods are essential for maintaining high standards in mask production, ultimately enhancing yield and reliability in semiconductor devices.</p></p>
