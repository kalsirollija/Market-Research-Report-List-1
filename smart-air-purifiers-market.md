<p><p>The smart air purifiers market features various technologies designed to enhance indoor air quality. HEPA filters capture small particles, allergens, and pollutants, making them highly effective for health-conscious users. Electrostatic precipitators charge particles for easy removal, ensuring efficient filtration without frequent replacements. Ionizers release negative ions that bond with airborne particles, promoting their settling. Lastly, ozone generators produce ozone to neutralize odors and pollutants; however, they can pose health risks if used improperly, making them less favored in residential settings.</p></p>
