<p><ul><li>Pretreatment</li><li>Cell Assembly</li><li>Post Processing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithium battery manufacturing equipment market consists of three main types: pretreatment, cell assembly, and post-processing. </p><p>Pretreatment equipment prepares raw materials by processing and refining components like lithium and other metals. Cell assembly machines then construct individual cells by stacking layers of anode, cathode, and separator materials. Finally, post-processing equipment involves curing, testing, and packaging the finished cells to ensure quality and performance. Together, these segments encompass the entire production lifecycle of lithium-ion batteries, crucial for various applications.</p></p>
