<p><p>Identity Management Systems (IMS) can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and web-based solutions. Cloud-based IMS are hosted on remote servers and offer greater scalability, flexibility, and accessibility, allowing users to manage identities from anywhere with internet access. In contrast, web-based IMS relies on a web browser interface, often providing easy access without extensive installations. Both types prioritize security and user management, but cloud-based solutions typically offer more advanced features such as automatic updates and integrated analytics.</p></p>
