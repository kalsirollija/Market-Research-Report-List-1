<p><p>The Rigid Intraocular Lens (IOL) market consists of various types that cater to different visual needs. Multifocal IOLs enable vision at multiple distances, reducing the need for glasses. Adjustable IOLs feature adjustable focus post-surgery, allowing personalized vision correction. Aspheric IOLs are designed to minimize spherical aberrations, enhancing visual clarity and contrast, especially in low-light conditions. Together, these innovations aim to improve patient outcomes and satisfaction following cataract surgeries or refractive lens exchange procedures.</p></p>
