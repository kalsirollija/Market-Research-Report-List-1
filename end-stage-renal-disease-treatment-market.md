<p><p>The End Stage Renal Disease (ESRD) treatment market encompasses three primary modalities: hemodialysis, peritoneal dialysis, and kidney transplantation. Hemodialysis involves filtering blood through a machine to remove waste, typically performed in clinics or at home. Peritoneal dialysis uses the lining of the abdominal cavity to filter blood, allowing for greater flexibility in treatment schedules. Kidney transplantation, a permanent solution, involves surgically replacing a diseased kidney with a healthy one from a donor, significantly improving patients' quality of life.</p></p>
