<p><p>The portable shredders market includes three primary types: strip-cut, cross-cut, and micro-cut shredders. Strip-cut shredders produce long strips of paper, offering basic security for personal use. Cross-cut shredders provide enhanced security by cutting paper into small confetti-like pieces, making it difficult to piece together. Micro-cut shredders offer the highest level of security, reducing documents into tiny fragments, ideal for sensitive data protection. Each type caters to different security needs and shredding volumes, targeting home and office users.</p></p>
