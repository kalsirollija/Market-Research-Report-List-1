<p><p>The Thermogravimetric Analyzer (TGA) market consists of two primary types: High Pressure Thermogravimetric Analyzers and Atmospheric Thermogravimetric Analyzers. High Pressure TGAs operate under elevated pressure conditions, enabling the analysis of materials that undergo reactions or phase changes at higher pressures, suitable for advanced research and industrial applications. Conversely, Atmospheric TGAs function under standard atmospheric pressure, primarily used for routine analysis of thermal stability and composition in various industries, including polymers, pharmaceuticals, and materials science.</p></p>
