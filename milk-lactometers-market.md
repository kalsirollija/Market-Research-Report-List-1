<p><p>The milk lactometer market comprises two primary types: ordinary lactometers and those equipped with thermometers. Ordinary lactometers measure the density of milk to determine its quality and purity, primarily relying on specific gravity. In contrast, lactometers with thermometers incorporate temperature measurement, allowing for more accurate readings by accounting for temperature variations that can affect density. Both types are essential tools in dairy testing, enabling producers to assess milk quality effectively and ensure compliance with health standards.</p></p>
