<p><p>The machine tool oils market encompasses various types, including hydraulic oil, mineral oil, and sideway oil. Hydraulic oil is essential for transferring power in hydraulic systems, ensuring efficient operation and lubrication. Mineral oil, derived from refining crude oil, is widely used for its excellent lubrication properties and stability. Sideway oil is specifically formulated for machine tool slideways, providing superior adhesion and reducing friction to enhance precision and performance. Together, these oils support the functionality and longevity of machine tools in industrial applications.</p></p>
