<p><p>The analgesics market consists of various types, primarily categorized into Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs) and central pain killers. NSAIDs, such as ibuprofen and aspirin, alleviate pain and reduce inflammation without corticosteroids. They are commonly used for conditions like arthritis and headaches. Central pain killers, including opioids like morphine and fentanyl, act on the central nervous system to relieve severe pain. While effective, they carry risks of dependence, making their use closely monitored in clinical settings.</p></p>
