<p><p>The Elevator Button Disinfection Device market comprises two primary types: Internal Elevator Button Sterilizers and External Elevator Button Sterilizers. Internal sterilizers are integrated within the elevator panel, automatically disinfecting buttons during operation. In contrast, external sterilizers are standalone devices positioned outside the elevator, allowing users to sanitize buttons before entering. Both types emphasize hygiene and safety, especially in high-traffic environments, to minimize the risk of surface transmission of pathogens and enhance user confidence in shared spaces.</p></p>
