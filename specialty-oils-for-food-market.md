<p><p>Specialty oils for food encompass various market types, including CBE (Chemical Blends and Extracts), CBS (Cold-Pressed and Steam Distilled), and others that cater to specific culinary needs. CBE oils are created through chemical processes and are often used for flavor enhancements. CBS oils are derived from cold-pressing or steam distillation, retaining natural flavors and nutritional properties. Other specialty oils can include infused or exotic oils, providing unique taste profiles and health benefits, appealing to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
