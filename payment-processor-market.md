<p><p>The payment processor market can be categorized into front-end and back-end services. Front-end payment processors focus on the customer interaction, facilitating transactions at the point of sale, online checkouts, or mobile payment systems. They handle tasks like payment authorization and user experience. In contrast, back-end processors manage the behind-the-scenes operations, including transaction settlement, payment routing, and risk management. Both types are essential for a seamless payment experience, ensuring secure and efficient transactions for businesses and consumers alike.</p></p>
