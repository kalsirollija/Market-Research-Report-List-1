<p><p>The genome engineering market comprises various technologies aimed at precise genetic modifications. CRISPR (Clustered Regularly Interspaced Short Palindromic Repeats) is widely used for its simplicity and efficiency in editing DNA. TALEN (Transcription Activator-Like Effector Nucleases) offers targeted gene editing with customizable capabilities. ZFN (Zinc Finger Nucleases) utilizes zinc finger proteins for gene manipulation. Antisense technology involves synthetic strands to inhibit gene expression. Other methods include use of RNA interference and base editing, expanding the diverse landscape of genome engineering tools.</p></p>
