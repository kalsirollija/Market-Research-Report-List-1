<p><strong>The Mask Reticle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quartz Mask</li><li>Soda Mask</li><li>Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Reticle Market comprises three main types: Quartz Masks, Soda Masks, and Film Masks. Quartz Masks are preferred for their high thermal stability and transparency in the photolithography process, making them ideal for semiconductor manufacturing. Soda Masks, made from soda-lime glass, are less expensive and suitable for lower-end applications. Film Masks, typically made from polymer materials, offer flexibility and are often used in prototyping and low-volume production. Each type caters to different needs within the semiconductor and electronics industries.</p></p>
