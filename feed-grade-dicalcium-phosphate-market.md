<p><p>The Feed Grade Dicalcium Phosphate market consists of three main processing types. By-product processing involves extracting DCP from phosphoric acid production that generates additional phosphates. Thermal phosphoric acid processing utilizes high-temperature reactions to produce phosphoric acid, yielding DCP as a by-product. Wet phosphoric acid processing involves the reaction of phosphate rock with sulfuric acid to create phosphoric acid, which is neutralized to form DCP. Each method impacts the quality, cost, and application of the final Dicalcium Phosphate product.</p></p>
