<p><p>The piezo actuators market comprises various types, each serving distinct applications. Multilayer actuators consist of several ceramic layers stacked together, providing high precision and force. Stacked actuators achieve high performance by layering multiple piezoelectric elements, ideal for applications demanding significant displacement. Shear actuators utilize shear deformation for linear motion, suitable for compact designs. Amplified actuators combine piezoelectric elements with mechanical amplification mechanisms, offering enhanced stroke and load capabilities, making them valuable in diverse industries such as robotics and optics.</p></p>
