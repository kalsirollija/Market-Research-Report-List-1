<p><ul><li>DOP</li><li>POP</li><li>MOP</li><li>POM</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Composite Film market is segmented into various types based on their properties and applications. DOP (Diffuser Optical Film) enhances light dispersion, improving uniformity in displays. POP (Polarizer Optical Film) focuses on controlling light polarization for better image clarity. MOP (Multi-layer Optical Film) provides advanced optical features through layered structures, enhancing performance in optical devices. POM (Prismatic Optical Film) utilizes prism technology to manipulate light angles for improved brightness. Other categories may include specialized films tailored for unique applications in various industries.</p></p>
