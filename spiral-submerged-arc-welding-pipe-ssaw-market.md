<p><p>Spiral Submerged Arc-Welding Pipe (SSAW) is a type of welded steel pipe made using a helical welding process. This method involves spirally winding a flat steel strip into a cylindrical shape, followed by welding along the seam using a submerged arc welding technique. SSAW pipes are known for their high strength, durability, and resistance to various environmental conditions, making them ideal for transporting oil, gas, and water. They are widely utilized in infrastructure projects, such as pipelines and construction.</p><p>The SSAW Pipe Market is experiencing significant growth due to rising demand for pipelines in the energy and water sectors.
