<p><strong>The Semiconductor Photoresist Developer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Developer</li><li>Negative Photoresist Developer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor photoresist developer market is primarily divided into two types: positive and negative photoresist developers. Positive photoresist developers work by removing the exposed areas of the photoresist after exposure to light, allowing the underlying material to be etched away. Conversely, negative photoresist developers harden the exposed areas, leaving behind the unexposed regions for removal. Both types play a crucial role in semiconductor fabrication, influencing resolution, sensitivity, and process efficiency in creating intricate circuit patterns on chips.</p></p>
