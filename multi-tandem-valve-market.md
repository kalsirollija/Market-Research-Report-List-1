<p><p>The Multi Tandem Valve market is characterized by two primary types: Integral Multi Tandem Valves and Shard Multi Tandem Valves. Integral Multi Tandem Valves feature a unified design, combining multiple valve functions into a single unit for enhanced efficiency and compactness, often used in hydraulic systems.
