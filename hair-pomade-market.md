<p><strong>The Hair Pomade Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Water-based Pomade"</li><li>"Oil-based Pomade"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hair pomade market comprises two main types: water-based and oil-based pomades. Water-based pomades offer easy application and wash out with water, making them popular for a lighter hold and natural finish. They typically dry quickly, allowing for restyling throughout the day. Conversely, oil-based pomades provide a stronger hold and a glossy finish, often lasting longer without the need for reapplication. However, they can be difficult to wash out and may weigh hair down.</p></p>
