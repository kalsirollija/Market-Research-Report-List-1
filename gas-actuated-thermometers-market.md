<p><p>Gas actuated thermometers are designed to measure temperature in various applications, and they come in two main types: remote mounted and panel mounted. Remote mounted gas actuated thermometers are installed away from the measurement site, allowing for safer or more convenient display, particularly in high-temperature or hazardous environments.
