<p><p>The incremental encoders market encompasses two primary types: incremental rotary encoders and incremental linear encoders. Incremental rotary encoders are used to measure the rotational position of a shaft, providing signals for speed and direction detection. In contrast, incremental linear encoders measure linear displacement, translating position changes into electrical signals. Both types play crucial roles in automation, robotics, and industrial applications, enhancing precision in movement control and feedback systems while facilitating efficient monitoring of machinery and processes.</p></p>
