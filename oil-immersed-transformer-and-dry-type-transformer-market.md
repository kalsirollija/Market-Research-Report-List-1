<p><p>Oil Immersed Transformers (OIT) and Dry Type Transformers (DTT) are two prominent categories of transformers used in electrical systems. OITs utilize oil as an insulating and cooling medium, providing excellent thermal management and enabling efficient operation in high-power applications. They are often preferred for their robust design and ability to handle higher loads, making them suitable for substations and industrial facilities. In contrast, DTTs use air for cooling and insulation, offering advantages such as reduced fire hazards and lower maintenance requirements. They are commonly employed in residential and commercial applications where space and safety are critical concerns.</p><p>The Oil Immersed Transformer and Dry Type Transformer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.9% during the forecast period.
