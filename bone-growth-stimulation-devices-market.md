<p><p>Bone growth stimulation devices utilize various technologies to enhance the healing of fractures and bone defects. Inductive coupling therapy employs electromagnetic fields to promote cellular activity and bone regeneration. Capacitive coupling therapy, on the other hand, uses electrical fields to stimulate osteogenesis through the mobilization of charged particles. Combined therapy integrates both inductive and capacitive coupling techniques to maximize therapeutic effects, offering a comprehensive approach to facilitate bone healing and recovery in patients with bone-related conditions.</p></p>
