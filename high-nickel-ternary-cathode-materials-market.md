<p><p>High-nickel ternary cathode materials, primarily NCM (Nickel Cobalt Manganese) and NCA (Nickel Cobalt Aluminum), are essential for advanced lithium-ion batteries. NCA, composed of nickel, cobalt, and aluminum, delivers higher energy density but can be more challenging in terms of stability and cost. Both materials drive advancements in battery technology, enhancing performance and efficiency.</p></p>
