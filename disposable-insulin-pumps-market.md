<p><ul><li>Closed-Loop</li><li>Open-Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable insulin pumps market is primarily categorized into closed-loop and open-loop systems. Closed-loop systems, often called artificial pancreas systems, automatically adjust insulin delivery based on real-time glucose levels, enhancing patient convenience and control. Open-loop systems require manual input and monitoring, where users must manually administer insulin based on their readings. Both types aim to improve diabetes management, but closed-loop systems offer a more automated solution, while open-loop systems provide flexibility for user management.</p></p>
