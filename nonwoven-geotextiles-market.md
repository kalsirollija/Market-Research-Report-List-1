<p><p>Nonwoven geotextiles are categorized into three main types based on their polymer composition: polypropylene (PP), polyethylene (PE), and polyester (PET). PP nonwovens are known for their durability and resistance to chemicals, making them suitable for drainage and filtration applications. PE nonwovens offer excellent waterproofing properties and are often used in erosion control and landfill projects. PET nonwovens provide high tensile strength and are commonly employed in reinforcement and separation functions. Together, these materials cater to diverse geotechnical needs.</p></p>
