<p><ul><li>Round Bodied Needles</li><li>Cutting Needles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiovascular needle market is primarily segmented into round-bodied needles and cutting needles. Round-bodied needles are designed for creating smooth and rounded punctures, minimizing trauma to delicate tissues, making them ideal for suturing or grafting. In contrast, cutting needles have a sharper, tri-beveled edge that allows for precise and efficient penetration through tougher tissues, commonly used in more complex cardiovascular procedures. Both types cater to specific surgical needs, enhancing outcomes in cardiovascular interventions.</p></p>
