<p><p>The transcriptomics market encompasses various technologies for analyzing gene expression. Microarrays enable the simultaneous measurement of thousands of genes, valuable for profiling expression in different conditions. Sequencing technologies, such as RNA-Seq, offer comprehensive insights into transcriptomes by reading entire RNA sequences, allowing for the identification of novel transcripts and alternative splicing events. Together, these methods drive advancements in genomics and personalized medicine.</p></p>
