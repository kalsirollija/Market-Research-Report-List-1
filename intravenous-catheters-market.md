<p><strong>The Intravenous Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Peripheral Catheters</li><li>Midline Peripheral Catheters</li><li>Central Venous Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intravenous catheters market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Peripheral Catheters** are short, thin tubes inserted into superficial veins, mainly for short-term medication delivery and fluid administration. </p><p>2. **Midline Peripheral Catheters** are longer than peripheral catheters, used for medium-term treatments, reaching deeper veins than peripheral options. </p><p>3. **Central Venous Catheters** are inserted into larger central veins, designed for long-term use, allowing for extended therapies, blood sampling, and more complex medication delivery. Each type serves specific clinical needs based on duration and complexity of treatment.</p></p>
