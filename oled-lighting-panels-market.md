<p><p>OLED lighting panels are categorized into two main types: flexible and rigid panels. Flexible panels are designed to bend and conform to various shapes, offering versatility in design and installation, making them ideal for innovative applications such as curved surfaces and portable devices. Rigid panels, on the other hand, offer stability and are typically used for traditional lighting fixtures and architectural applications. Both types leverage OLED technology to provide energy-efficient, high-quality illumination with thinner profiles and improved aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
