<p><ul><li>Iron Core Linear Motors</li><li>Iron Less Linear Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear motor modules are vital in automation and precision movement applications. The market is primarily divided into two types: iron core linear motors and ironless linear motors. </p><p>Iron core linear motors utilize a ferromagnetic core, providing high force and efficiency but may generate more heat and noise. In contrast, ironless linear motors feature a coreless design that results in lower weight, reduced cogging, and smoother operation, making them ideal for high-speed, precision applications. Both types cater to varied industrial needs.</p></p>
